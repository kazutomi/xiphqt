Module: pagestat

Displays rate of disk pages pulled in by the kernel/written out by the kernel.

Current platforms

Linux

Instances

single only

additional resources

none

Resource defaults

Resource defaults explicitly altered from the global defaults by this module:
  • bar.0.label: page in
  • bar.1.label: page out
  • bar.0.litbackground: #e7ad74
  • bar.1.litbackground: #ade774
  • order: 12
  • scalelenadj: 60
  • scalereturn: 120
  • scalewidadj: 160

Possible enhancements

Could (and should) be extended to use vmstat/iostat utilities on platforms other than Linux (eg, Solaris *BSD).
